Design

Through courses such as Human Experience in Design and Communication Design Fundamentals as well as my internship experience at RetailMeNot, I am continuing to develop as a designer. I am also trained in HCI Methods that are essential to creating and testing more usable experiences. Whether through user experience design, product design, or visual design, I am continually driven to create immersive and intuitive experiences.

Research

Data collection and research are just as important as the act of designing. I believe that combining principles of cognitive science and perception with principles of design can lead to extraordinary innovation. It is through this lens that I have approached my work with Disney Research and developed my skills in courses such as Learning in Humans and Machines and Research Methods in Cognitive Psychology.

Programming

Without implementation, design loses its power. My experience with imperative and functional programming enable me to understand constraints and even implement designs on my own if necessary. I am proficient with Python, Ruby, C, SML, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Processing, MAX, Arduino, and SQL, which helps me to choose and understand the best tools to use for any particular project.
