Course: HCI 440 - Introduction to User Centered Design

      user experience design

PROJECT BACKGROUND

Students were given 2 hours in class to come up with a mobile app concept and sketched wireframes that would address the below prompt:

"On a recent Friday in Boulder I packed up my bag for work, got my son ready for school and went out the door. It was 50 degrees and sunny out, and I dressed appropriately. I was inside most of the day in meetings and didn’t pay much attention to what was happening outside. When I left work to catch the bus on my way home it was raining, 30 degrees, and the temperature was dropping fast. I was freezing and soaked by the time I got home. Clearly I could have planned my day better."

The final deliverables were an explanation of our design process and app, as well as low-fidelity wireframes.

DESIGN PROCESS

• Researched top weather app competitors within the iTunes app store

• Created user personas based on prompt given by professor, determined use scenarios and flows for these users

• Ideated designs and sketched wireframes

POST CLASS

• Created high-fidelity app wireframes

• Annotated wireframes

• Built presentation deck
