DonorPath

UX and UI Design

COMPANY AND PROJECT BACKGROUND

DonorPath is an online application that pairs nonprofits with a fundraising expert who then uses their donor data to increase fundraising potential.

The original request for the project was to focus on a visual design overhaul of the registration process due to the lack of branding consistency between a user registering and the marketing website. Upon evaluating the registration process, I realized that not only was there visual work to be done, but that the user experience needed to be tweaked.

DESIGN PROCESS

• I performed a heuristic evaluation to determine the most pressing experience issues and redesigned the process accordingly. My main findings for the first round of redesign revolved around help and documentation, visbility of system status as well as user control and freedom.

• Sketched options for the process

• Created low fidelity prototypes for A and B versions of the process

• Utilized UserTesting.com to determine a winner between the two designs

• Created high fidelity wireframes and spec sheets
