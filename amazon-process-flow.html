Process Flow

Information Architecture

ASSIGNMENT

Students enrolled in HCI 454: Interaction Design and Information Architecture were asked to choose a process and describe its elements and a user's interaction graphically through a process flow diagram as well as provide a brief description of the flow.

DELIVERABLE

• When a user adds an item to their basket and decides to check out, they are first prompted to sign into their account

• Once a user has signed in, they are directed to choose or provide a shipping address for their item

• Once complete, users then move on to selecting a payment method. Here they can choose from previously used credit/debit cards, adding a new credit/debit card, using a bank account, among many other options

• After a payment method is chosen, users are then taken to the final page of the checkout process where they can review their order and make any changes to their selected shipping and payment methods as well as place their order
