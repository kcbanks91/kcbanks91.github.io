My Design Process

Define goals and strategy

Strategy is important from the outset of any project because it will shape the goals, what the organization is hoping to achieve with the project, how its success should be measured, and what priority it should have in the grand scheme of things.

Research

The research phase is key to creating an informed user experience. This phase of the approach includes user interviews, observations, and surveys.

Analysis

This is where I draw insights from the data collected during the research phase. This part of the process usually helps produce user personas and user scenarios.
