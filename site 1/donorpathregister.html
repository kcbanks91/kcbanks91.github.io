registration redesign

BACKGROUND

DonorPath is an online application that pairs nonprofits with a fundraising expert who then uses their donor data to increase fundraising potential.

PROCESS

Originally I was asked to do a visual redesign so as to carry the DonorPath brand experience through to the registration process. Upon evaluating the process, I realized that not only was there visual work to be done, but that the user experience needed to be tweaked.

I performed a heuristic evaluation to determine the most pressing experience issues and redesigned the process accordingly.

Use the sliders on the images below to see portions of the redesign. Actual pages can be found via the DonorPath website
