RetailMeNot

UX Design Concepting and Usability Testing

PROJECT OVERVIEW

For the 2015 summer internship program, the RetailMeNot Product interns were given the task to brainstorm and pitch an idea for the company's 2016 planning roadmap.

After researching and discussing multiple options with product managers and executives, we pitched the idea to include a feature to upload physical gift cards into the RetailMeNot application.

PROCESS

• Brainstormed project ideas and did preliminary feasibility, financial implication, and competitive research on these ideas

• Determined user process flows for both adding and using a gift card while taking into account the current and proposed architecture for the app

• Created lo-fi wireframe screens for both processes

• Pitched project (including financials and lo-fi wireframes) to members of executive team for feedback

• Worked with mentor to iterate on designs based on comments/concerns from presentation

• Created usability test through UserTesting.com for first round of testing

• Iterated on designs based on user feedback

• Prepared prototype for additional usability testing using a third party moderator

• Compiled notes from usability and prepared next step documentation for final executive presentation
