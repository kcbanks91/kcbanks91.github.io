RetailMeNot

UX Design Concepting and Usability Testing

BACKGROUND

RetailMeNot, Inc. operates the world's largest marketplace for digital coupon offers. The company enables consumers across the globe to find hundreds of thousands of digital offers for their favorite retailers and brands.

As RetailMeNot and its offerings have grown, additional information has been identified as potentially relevant to consumers for the coupon offer modals. My project was to ideate and create mobile web designs to complement the desktop full-page coupon, sale, and product landing experience concept designs.

DESIGN AND TESTING PROCESS

• Took inventory of different types of modals currently in use on the RetailMeNot desktop and mobile properties. Determined what information was necessary and what was lacking on each modal

• Completed a cognitive walkthrough for the coupon code redemption process

• Worked with product manager to determine potential additional elements to include for new landing pages

• Ideated and sketched multiple variations of the mobile web landing experience

• Built medium-fidelity mobile prototype for usability testing

• Wrote usability test plan for both desktop and mobile web and coordinated the testing process with a third party moderator

• Compiled notes from usability and discussed next steps and further iterations with members of the design team
