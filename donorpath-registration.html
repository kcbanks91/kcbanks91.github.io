COMPANY BACKGROUND

DonorPath is an online application that pairs nonprofits with a fundraising expert who then uses their donor data to increase fundraising potential.

PROCESS

The original request for the project was to focus on a visual design overhaul of the registration process due to the lack of branding consistency between the process and the marketing website. Upon evaluating the process, I realized that not only was there visual work to be done, but that the user experience needed to be tweaked.

I performed a heuristic evaluation to determine the most pressing experience issues and redesigned the process accordingly. My main findings for the first round of redesign revolved around help and documentation, visbility of system status as well as user control and freedom.

Use the sliders on the images below to see portions of the redesign. Actual pages can be found via the DonorPath website.
